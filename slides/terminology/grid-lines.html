Grid Lines

  • The vertical and horizontal lines that divide the grid and separate the columns and rows.
  • Grid lines are numbered left to right and top to bottom.
  • They respect the language direction, so if you're coming from an RLT language, they will also be numbered accordingly.
  • Grid columns and rows are not numbered by the actual column or row itself, but by the lines that start and stop them.
  • Because of that, you'll always have one more line number than you actually have column or row, which is useful to know when placing items, as we'll see in a bit.
